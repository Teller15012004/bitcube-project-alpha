## Proposed Team Velocity
    A realistic Agile team velocity for a small to medium-sized development team is approximately 12–15 story points per sprint.

    For this project, a velocity of 13 story points per sprint is reasonable because:
    - Some stories involve complex scheduling and conflict validation logic
    - The team must handle both frontend and backend development
    - Testing booking workflows and edge cases requires additional effort
    - Administrative reporting features add technical complexity

## Estimated Number of Sprints
Using a velocity of 13 story points:
52 total story points ÷ 13 points per sprint = 4 sprints

Administrative analytics and conflict resolution are scheduled later because they rely on stable booking data and completed workflows.
