### Key Learning

### Assumptions That Failed
    -The assumption that broke down most was an estimation assumption.

### Behavioural Change
    -n Sprint 1, the dependency risk between Stories #1 and #4 was visible in standups from day 6 onward. I was aware that Story #1 was running longer than expected. But I treated that awareness as passive information rather than an active signal requiring a response. I noted it, updated the board, and moved on. I did not escalate it, I did not flag Story #4 as at-risk, and I did not propose a replan.

### Professional Growth
    -The skill I need to strengthen is structured sprint communication — specifically, knowing what information matters, when it needs to be said, and how to say it without it feeling like an escalation or blame.
